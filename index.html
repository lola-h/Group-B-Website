Where Should I Donate?

Red Cross

More than 9,000 Turkish Red Crescent (Kizilay) and Syrian Arab Red Crescent staff and volunteers have been working around the clock in unimaginably difficult conditions to help people who have lost everything. The best way you can help those affected by the earthquakes is to donate to the Turkey-Syria Earthquake Appeal.

Al Mustafa

Al Mustafa Welfare Trust has been on the ground, working in Turkey & with Syrian refugees for many years. As desperate recovery efforts continue, we need your support to deliver urgent emergency aid to those affected by the earthquake.

Muslim Hands

With your support we will be constructing 50 container homes as part of our emergency relief efforts in Türkiye which already includes providing hot food, clothing and blankets. These homes will form part of a container home city, helping to house some of the most vulnerable families in Hatay, which is one of the hardest hit areas in Türkiye.
