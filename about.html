About Us

We are a non-profitable organisation committed to providing relief and support to those affected by the 7.8 magnitude earthquake that struck Turkey and Syria on Monday 6th of February.

Share this page

Our Mission

Our mission is to aid and support to the victims of the Turkey-Syria earthquake with shelter, food, medical care and other necessities. We believe that everyone deserves access to basic human needs, and that every little bit helps; therefore, we will inspire people to respond rapidly to disasters and fight poverty through our values and expertise.

We have years of experience in disaster relief and humanitarian aid, and we know with your support, we can make a real impact. We will be providing regular updates on our website and social media platforms to keep everyone informed about our progress.

Thank you for considering donating to our cause. With your help, we can provide much needed support to those affected by the Turkey-Syria earthquake and help them rebuild their lives.
